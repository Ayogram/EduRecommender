Understanding Your Recommendation Metrics

🎯 Precision Measures the accuracy of our suggestions. It is the percentage of recommended courses that directly match your department, academic field, or interests.
🔍 Recall Measures the coverage of the engine. It is the percentage of all matching courses in the system that the AI successfully surfaced.
⚖️ F1 Score The overall rating of recommendation performance. It is the harmonic mean of Precision and Recall, representing the balance between accuracy and coverage.
